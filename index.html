Example in Mozilla Firefox:

Patchnotes for Version 1.3.2:
New:

  • Emojis can be disabled.
  • Changed the appearance of the option buttons.
  • You can see the votes in the userlist.
  • Settings will now be saved.
  • Staff members now have their respective icon in the list.
  • Added Check History to the options.
  • You will now get a notification when it is your turn.

Current features:

  • Automatic woot or waitlist join. Can be turned off. (Bouncers and higher can disable autjoin with "@username !disable")
  • Shows a log of playing tracks and their votes and curates. The log can be hidden.
  • Lists all online users and users in the waitlist on small space.
  • Awaybot that replies with a specified message when you're being mentioned.
  • Displays a message at which place a user left the waitlist or dj booth.
  • Shows how many users left the waitlist or dj booth during a song.
  • Historybot that notifies you if the current song is in the history while the bot is active.
  • Annotations when somebody leaves or joins the room.
  • You get notified when your song is up.
  • Shows votes in the userlist.
  • Ability do disable Emojis.

Tested in Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome.
